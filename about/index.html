Michal Orman is a passionate of Software Development and Software Engineering since a very long time. He constantly is looking for new ways of solving problems more efficiently and in a more elegant way. He loves good design, clean and elegant code. He strongly believes in picking the right tools for the right problems.

He started with programming in Pascal with a little bit of Assembler in high school. Then he continued with C into an Object Oriented world of programming first in C++ then Java. He had a small episode with mobile programming for Android Platform and now he works with Ruby and Rails on a daily basis. Lately his interest is focused around node.js.

Michal is an evangelist of Agile and strongly believes in the agile approach for software development. He's constantly trying to make business and developers to work in a better and more productive environment. Delivering great products is what motivates him the most.

His weapons of choice are Ubuntu Linux as opearting system, IntelliJ IDEA for Java/Android development, Vim and tmux for Ruby/Rails/HTML/JavaScript development and Git for version controlling. He loves to work with lightweight frameworks and lightning-fast and highly customizable tools.

Michal is certified PRINCE2 Practitioner, Certified Professional Java Programmer, Professional Scrum Master, and Professional Scrum Product Owner. He is the owner of the NEOSOFT Orman Michal (and its only employee ;)). He collaborate with Consileon Polska as IT consultant, Project/Technical/Team Leader, Architect/Designer, Lead Developer, Analyst (depending on project he plays different roles). He is also involved in Consileon's growth by collaboration in defining company's vission, strategies, processes, QA, knowledge management, and ways the Consileon should follow. So he is much more than just software developer.
